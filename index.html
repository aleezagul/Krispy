About Us

Welcome to Krispy Chicken, your go-to destination for delicious takeaway in Levenshulme, Manchester! Located at 906 Stockport Rd, we pride ourselves on serving a diverse menu that caters to all taste buds. From our mouthwatering chicken wings to our flavorful burgers and hearty platters, each dish is crafted with care and attention to detail, ensuring a scrumptious experience with every bite.

At Krispy Chicken, we believe that great food brings people together. Our wide variety of offerings, including crispy nachos and a selection of signature platters, is perfect for sharing with family and friends or indulging on your own. We’re committed to using high-quality ingredients and delivering a memorable dining experience for our customers.

Explore our menu and order directly from our website to enjoy a taste of Manchester’s finest takeaway. Join the Krispy Chicken family and savor the flavors that have made us a local favorite!
